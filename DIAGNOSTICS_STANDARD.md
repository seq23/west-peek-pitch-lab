# Diagnostics Standard

Tier 2 and Tier 3 failures, plus approval-overlay reviews write run-scoped evidence under `artifacts/diagnostics/<run_id>/<test_id>/`: runtime context, request/response, final URL, console/network, persistence, provider evidence, screenshots/trace, cleanup, retryability, redaction, completion impact. No secrets, tokens, cookies, unnecessary PII, or private payloads.

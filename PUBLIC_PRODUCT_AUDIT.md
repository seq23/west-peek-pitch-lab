# Public Product Audit — West Peek Pitch Lab

## Audit persona

Evaluate first as a founder who knows nothing, then as a senior UX/product reviewer. A valid release must answer within five seconds: what this is, what the founder gets, whether it is private, and what to do first.

## Hard usability questions

1. Is `Start Step 1` fully visible at 390×844 and 1440×900?
2. Does the CTA lead directly to a visible profile task rather than another landing page?
3. Does Scooter remain present without displacing the active work?
4. Are profile, deck, question, draft, review, and share states progressively disclosed?
5. Is the draft hidden before meaningful input and rewarding after input?
6. Does desktop preserve conversation-first hierarchy and mobile use a real bottom sheet?
7. Are navigation, focus, labels, touch targets, and overflow safe?
8. Is `Founder Story Card` the single active product name?
9. Is all provider, caching, duration, version, and release-contract language absent from public surfaces?
10. Do consent and confirmed-receipt states remain truthful?

## Completion boundary

Container structural proof is not real browser proof. Local headed Playwright, route/state Hallmark evidence, deployed click audit, applicable provider mutation/readback, exact cleanup, and post-cleanup integrity are required for COMPLETE.

## Non-negotiable product invariant

Pitch Lab must feel like a short coaching session with AI Scooter. Scooter speaks at the opening, at the final personalized coaching summary, and at the share decision. Cost control is achieved through cached clips, short scripts, selected dynamic moments, render caps, and honest degraded fallback — not by turning Scooter into a static mascot or generic chatbot.

This document is the disclosure register for West Peek Pitch Lab. The live site should implement these user-facing disclosures, and Playwright tests should prove that the important disclosures are visible.

## Footer links

The footer must include `/terms`, `/privacy`, `/ai-disclosure`, `/founder-network-notice`, `/data-consent`, `/contact`, and `/delete-my-info`.

## Policy page requirements

Terms must explain pitch practice, no professional advice, no guaranteed outcomes, user responsibility, acceptable use, AI limitations, and West Peek rights to decline or ignore submissions.

Privacy must explain profile data, founder answers, optional deck context, consent logs, local-only recording, what may be shared with West Peek, deletion/update path, AI/provider processing, cookies/local storage.

AI Disclosure must state AI Scooter is AI, not real Scooter, not human review, outputs may be incomplete/inaccurate, and no professional advice is provided.

Founder Network Notice must state optional network sharing, relationship-routing framing, no guaranteed follow-up, not an investment application, not an offer to invest, and not solicitation to buy or sell securities.

Data & Consent must separate profile capture from share consent, deck file consent, future video consent, and the update/resubmit process.
